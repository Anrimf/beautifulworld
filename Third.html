Beautiful World: Chapters

Prologue

I spend most of my time observing the beauty of my surroundings. I stare at texture, form, shadows, values, shapes, and negative space, and start drawing with black ink or pens without sketching with a pencil. I follow the flow of the location, dream depth, or living thing with detail and perception.

Chapter 01: Where should I go?

Dream #01: Miami, Florida- USA

Field of petals with a path broken by the unknown girl in the distance. Straight to a tornado. She won't look back; proceed forward. Even with strong winds, the girl is fearless and determined to confront what the future holds. The dream is sometimes repeated in the same scene. Other times, the field is fogged. As I reached out my hand towards the girl; I had awoken on the cold tile floor with no memory of falling off the bed.
